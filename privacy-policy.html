Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18th March 2026

At Emergency Electrician Dublin, we respect your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimalism. We do not automatically collect data from you when you visit our website.

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us. This usually occurs when you fill out a contact form, request a quote, or email us directly. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, physical address, and details regarding your electrical project or emergency.

2. Cookies

We do not use cookies, pixels, or any third-party tracking analytics (such as Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel) on this website. We do not track your browsing history or behavior across our site.

3. How We Use Your Information

If you choose to contact us, we use your information strictly for the following purposes:

  • To dispatch an electrician for emergency call-outs.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide estimates or quotes.
  • To schedule services and perform electrical installations or repairs.
  • To communicate with you regarding your project.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties. We do not sell or rent your information to other companies.
